My Story

by Annabella Leuzze

I was born in Mountain View, California on August 1st, 2002. I grew up in the Evergreen suburbs in San Jose. I attended Silver Oak Elementary School, Chaboya Middle School, and Archbishop Mitty High School. In middle school, I played soccer and ran cross country and track. In high school, I played soccer and ran cross country until I tore my ACL, MCL, and mensicus all at once. After this injury I joined the speech and debate team.

My places of employment as a teenager were as follows:

  • Rockin' Jump Trampoline Park
  • Jamba Juice
  • Black Angus Steakhouse

My favorite pizza place from home is Bibo's NY Pizza. My dad is friends with the owner's brother. Check out their website here: Bibo's NY Pizza

My parents made the decision to move to Fort Collins, Colorado in the summer of 2021.

My freshman year at Syracuse University I lived in Day Hall. This year I am living in Watson Hall.
